# Candidate E integration

Candidate E is preserved in the final repository as an optional deeper operating mode.

## What Candidate E changes

Compared with profile D, Candidate E increases planning depth, produces deeper handoff structure, expands review packet depth, and creates stronger D-versus-E comparison artifacts.

## What Candidate E does not change

Candidate E does not become the default profile.

Candidate E does not enable silent auto-apply.

Candidate E does not bypass review-first behavior.

Candidate E does not turn warning or residue handling into hidden behavior.

## Where the proof lives

## Final repository stance

The final repository preserves the Candidate E code and proof surfaces without restoring the old clutter-heavy imported repository mirrors.
