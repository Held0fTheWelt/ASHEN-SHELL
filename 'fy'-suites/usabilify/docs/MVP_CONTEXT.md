# Usabilify in the MVP Context

`usabilify` is the UI and usability governance suite in the autark `fy` workspace.

It sits next to `templatify`, `documentify`, `docify`, and `contractify`.

Its job is not to redesign the product. Its job is to make UI and UX quality visible,
readable, and actionable:

- inventory UI surfaces
- connect them to relevant contracts and guidance
- score them with stable usability rules
- produce simple next-step reports
- support template normalization through `templatify`
- support documentation growth through `documentify`

This makes `usabilify` the bridge between technical template structure and human-facing usability quality.
